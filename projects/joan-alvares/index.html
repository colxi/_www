JOAN ALVARES : PORTFOLIO

Development of the personal portfolio of Joan Alvares. The webpage is powered by Wordpress, for versatile content management in the back-end, and a minimal custom template based in AJAX, to achieve a single-page web experience. The whole webpage follows the typographic highly stuctured style that characterizes the identity and trajectory of this creative.
